## Test 1

### Q1. The soil formation process is termed as:

- A. Pedogenesis
- B. Gametogenesis
- C. Caryogenesis
- D. None of the options

**Correct Answer:** Pedogenesis

**Why:** Pedogenesis is the scientific term for the natural process by which soil forms from weathered parent material and organic matter.

### Q2. Which of the following is also known as "illuviation zone"?

- A. A-horizon
- B. B-horizon
- C. O-horizon
- D. None of the options

**Correct Answer:** B-horizon

**Why:** The B-horizon is the illuviation zone because minerals and clay leached from upper layers accumulate there.

### Q3. What is 'soil erosion'?

- A. Process by which soil is formed
- B. Harmful process that involves the removal & transportation of soil by wind & water
- C. Process often referred to as the 'greenhouse' effect
- D. None of the options

**Correct Answer:** Harmful process that involves the removal & transportation of soil by wind & water

**Why:** Soil erosion means the removal and transport of soil, especially topsoil, by agents such as wind and water.

### Q4. What effect can soil have on health, if eaten or inhaled?

- A. Nothing - it is perfectly safe
- B. Can be good for your health
- C. Can have serious health implications such as cancer
- D. None of the options

**Correct Answer:** Can have serious health implications such as cancer

**Why:** Contaminated soil may contain toxic metals, chemicals, or pathogens, so eating or inhaling it can cause serious health effects including cancer.

### Q5. The following is popularly known as the age of dinosaurs:

- A. paleozoic
- B. mesozoic
- C. coenozoic
- D. none of the options

**Correct Answer:** mesozoic

**Why:** The Mesozoic era is popularly called the age of dinosaurs because dinosaurs were the dominant land animals then.

### Q6. Natural hazards include:

- A. earthquakes
- B. floods
- C. forest fires
- D. all of the options

**Correct Answer:** all of the options

**Why:** Earthquakes, floods, and forest fires are all naturally occurring hazardous events.

### Q7. The following are natural resources:

- A. soil
- B. water
- C. minerals
- D. all of the options

**Correct Answer:** all of the options

**Why:** Soil, water, and minerals are all natural resources because they occur in nature and are useful to humans and ecosystems.

### Q8. The following are climatic factors:

- A. humidity
- B. rainfall
- C. temperature
- D. all of the options

**Correct Answer:** all of the options

**Why:** Humidity, rainfall, and temperature are climatic factors because they describe atmospheric conditions over time.
